About our team

"The ASME SJCET team is a vibrant and dynamic group of students and faculty from SJCET, passionate about engineering excellence and innovation. As a part of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME), this team embodies the spirit of collaboration, creativity, and problem-solving. With a focus on developing cutting-edge solutions and promoting STEM education, the ASME SJCET team engages in various projects, competitions, and community outreach initiatives.
